Our Mission

The Everest Humanitarian Trust supports single parents in their journey towards self-sufficiency. We provide flexible job opportunities, fundraising assistance for business ventures, establish daycare facilities and donate to orphanages, to help single parents achieve financial stability and provide opportunities for success.

We also aim to provide support, create a supportive environment for children, give back to the community, and reduce the number of divorce cases through counseling. Our goal is to work towards a brighter future for all.

Services

Employment Opportunity for Single Parent

The Everest Humanitarian Trust is committed to helping single parents find flexible and convenient employment opportunities. We understand that finding work can be challenging for those raising children alone, which is why we work with local businesses and organizations to match single parents with job openings that suit their skills and qualifications. We also provide support for job interviews, resume building, and entrepreneurial pursuits. Our goal is to empower single parents to achieve financial stability, independence and provide for their families, also building a better future for themselves and their children.

Help Fundraising for Entrepreneurial Pursuits for Single Parent

The Everest Humanitarian Trust is dedicated to helping single parents achieve their entrepreneurial goals by providing financial support and resources for their business ventures. We connect them with potential investors and grant opportunities and offer guidance on how to create a successful crowdfunding campaign. Our goal is to empower single parents to achieve financial stability and independence through their own business ventures and create a brighter future for themselves and their families.

Day Care Facilities for Children

The Everest Humanitarian Trust mission is to provide day care facilities for children of single parents to make their task of raising children a little easier. Our facilities will be staffed by experienced professionals, providing a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment for children while their parents work. We will also offer a variety of activities and educational programs, and support services such as parenting classes and counseling services. Our ultimate goal is to empower single parents by creating a supportive and nurturing environment for both children and their parents, helping build stronger, healthier families and creating a brighter future for all.

Counselling Centre to reduce Divorce Cases

The Everest Humanitarian Trust is committed to reducing the number of divorce cases by setting up counseling centers for couples. Our centers will be staffed by trained professionals who can help couples resolve conflicts and communicate better. Services offered include individual therapy, couples therapy, and group counseling. We also aim to provide workshops and educational programs that teach couples new skills and techniques for building a healthy relationship. These include topics such as conflict resolution, effective communication, and maintaining intimacy and connection. Our goal is to empower couples to strengthen their relationships and create a brighter future for all.

Scholarships for Children

The Everest Humanitarian Trust provides scholarships for the children of single parents to help them achieve their educational and career goals. We believe every child should have the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their family circumstances. We partner with schools, universities and organizations to provide financial assistance and support services such as tutoring, academic coaching, mentorship programs, and access to internships and job shadowing opportunities. Our goal is to empower the children of single parents to build a better future for themselves and ultimately create a brighter future for all.

Group Insurance Facilities for Single Parents and Children

The Everest Humanitarian Trust aims to provide group health and life insurance to single parents and their children to ensure access to quality healthcare and financial protection. We offer affordable and comprehensive insurance coverage to all, regardless of family circumstances. This is achieved through partnerships with insurance companies for group rates and discounts, as well as resources and support for understanding insurance options. Our goal is to provide coverage and support for single parents and children to stay healthy and financially secure. Investing in the well-being of single parents and families creates a brighter future for all.
